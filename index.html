About me

Hello there!
I'm Saurabh Dhuri, an aspiring UX Designer and Product Designer, who is passionate about creating User-Centered Designs.

Currently, I'm thriving as a UI Designer, embracing a steep learning curve and actively seeking new opportunities to immerse myself further in the world of UX & Product Design.
With over 2 years of experience in UI Design, my expertise lies in visual design, interaction design, wireframing, and prototyping, with a strong focus on accessibility and inclusivity. My ultimate goal is to build a fulfilling career in UX, where I can combine my passion for design with my problem-solving skills.

I am excited about working on impactful projects that not only delight users visually but also address real-world challenges. Rather than being just another Designer, I aspire to be recognized as a Problem Solver who makes a meaningful difference in the industry.
